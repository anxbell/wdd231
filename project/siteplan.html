Site Purpose

The purpose of the Sadie's Flower Shop site is to provide a comprehensive platform for showcasing a diverse range of flower arrangements and related products. The site will feature product listings with detailed descriptions, pricing, and high-quality images of the flowers. Additionally, it will offer information on flower care, arrangement ideas, and seasonal specials to inspire customers.

Scenarios

Browse Flower Arrangements: Customers can easily navigate through various categories of flower arrangements, from bouquets to centerpieces, each accompanied by stunning visuals and descriptions.

Special Offers and Events: Feature promotions, seasonal collections, and event announcements to keep customers engaged and informed about new offerings.

Inspiration Gallery: A dedicated section featuring an inspiration gallery with photos from previous weddings. This allows couples to browse different styles and arrangements, helping them visualize their own wedding flowers.
