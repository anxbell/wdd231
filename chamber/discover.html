Welcome to the San Salvador Chamber of Commerce

We are committed to supporting local businesses, fostering economic growth, and creating a thriving business community in San Salvador. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs, connect businesses, and strengthen the local economy.

Our History

Founded in 1921, the Chamber of Commerce has been a key player in the economic development of San Salvador. With nearly a century of experience, we have grown to represent hundreds of businesses across various sectors. We are proud of our contributions to making San Salvador a better place to live and work.
